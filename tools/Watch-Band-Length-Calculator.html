How to Measure

  1. Use a flexible measuring tape or a piece of string
  2. Wrap around your wrist where you normally wear your watch
  3. Keep the tape snug but not tight
  4. Measure at the end of the day when wrists are slightly swollen
  5. Measure over the bone for the most accurate fit

Tip: For comfort, add 1-2mm extra to your measurement.
