Pet Boarding Cost Information

Planning for pet boarding costs is essential for travel preparation:

  • Prices vary significantly by location and service level
  • Peak seasons and holidays often have surcharges
  • Additional services increase total costs
  • Early booking may offer discounts
Important: This calculator provides estimates only. Actual prices vary by facility, location, and services. Contact facilities directly for accurate pricing and availability. Book early for peak travel periods.

Dog boarding typically costs $25-85 per night depending on size and services. Cat boarding averages $20-50 per night. Luxury facilities can exceed $100 per night. Many facilities offer discounts for extended stays or multiple pets.
