Claw Trimming Information

Regular claw trimming is essential for your pet's health and comfort:

  • Prevents overgrown nails that can cause pain
  • Reduces risk of nail breakage and injury
  • Protects furniture and surfaces
  • Maintains proper gait and posture
Important: This timer provides general trimming recommendations. Be careful not to cut the quick (blood vessel inside the nail). If you're unsure, consult your veterinarian or professional groomer. Some pets may require more frequent trimming.

Most pets need claw trimming every 2-4 weeks. Cats typically need more frequent trimming than dogs. Use proper pet nail clippers and have styptic powder on hand in case of bleeding. Start slowly with nervous pets.
