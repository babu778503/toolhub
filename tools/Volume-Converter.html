About Volume Units

The liter (L) is a metric unit of volume equal to one cubic decimeter (dm³). The gallon (US) is a unit of liquid capacity equal to 231 cubic inches. The cubic meter (m³) is the SI unit of volume, equivalent to 1000 liters.
