Tea Steeping Tips

  • Green tea: 70-80°C for 2-3 minutes to avoid bitterness
  • Black tea: 95-100°C for 3-5 minutes for full flavor
  • Use filtered water for the best taste
  • Steep covered to retain essential oils and aroma
  • Remove tea bag/leaves when timer ends to prevent over-steeping
  • For iced tea, double the amount of tea and pour over ice
  • Adjust steeping time based on your taste preference
