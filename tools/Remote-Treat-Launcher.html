Remote Treat Launcher Information

Remote treat launching enhances pet training and interaction:

  • Reinforces positive behaviors instantly
  • Allows training from a distance
  • Provides mental stimulation for pets
  • Strengthens bond between pet and owner
Important: This launcher simulator demonstrates treat dispensing concepts. For actual remote treat dispensing, use a compatible smart feeder or treat launcher device. Always use healthy, appropriately sized treats. Monitor your pet for signs of overeating.

Remote treat launchers work best with small, lightweight treats that won't jam the mechanism. Training treats are ideal because they're designed for frequent dispensing. Dry kibble works well in most dispensers. Soft treats may require special dispensers to prevent sticking.
