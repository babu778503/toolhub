GPA Calculation Formulas

Weighted GPA

Formula:
Σ(Grade Points × Credits) ÷ Total Credits

Example:
(3.7×3 + 4.0×4 + 3.0×2) ÷ (3+4+2) = 3.52

Unweighted GPA

Formula:
Σ(Grade Points) ÷ Number of Courses

Example:
(3.7 + 4.0 + 3.0) ÷ 3 = 3.57

Scale Conversion

4.0 to 5.0:
GPA × 1.25

5.0 to 4.0:
GPA × 0.8

Percentage:
(GPA × 25) + 55

GPA Tips & Strategies

Maintain High GPA

  • Attend all classes regularly
  • Take detailed notes
  • Complete assignments on time
  • Participate in discussions
  • Seek help when needed

Improve Your GPA

  • Retake courses if allowed
  • Focus on challenging courses
  • Join study groups
  • Use campus resources
  • Stay organized

GPA Requirements

  • Honors: 3.5+ GPA
  • Dean's List: 3.7+ GPA
  • Graduate School: 3.0+ GPA
  • Medical School: 3.5+ GPA
  • Law School: 3.0+ GPA

Conversion Tips

  • Check institutional scales
  • Understand weighted vs unweighted
  • Consider plus/minus grading
  • Account for different systems
  • Verify conversion accuracy
