Boiling Tips

  • Water boils at 100°C (212°F) at sea level
  • Boiling point decreases ~1°C every 300m of elevation
  • Use a lid to reduce boiling time by up to 50%
  • Add salt after water starts boiling for better flavor
  • Start timing once you see steady bubbling
  • Electric kettles boil water faster than stovetop
