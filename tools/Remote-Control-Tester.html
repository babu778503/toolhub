Remote Control Troubleshooting

  • Check batteries first - replace if weak or dead
  • Clean the IR emitter window with a soft cloth
  • Ensure there's a clear line of sight to the device
  • Test with another device to isolate the problem
  • Reset the remote by removing batteries for 30 seconds
