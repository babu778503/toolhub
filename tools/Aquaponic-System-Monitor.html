Aquaponic System Information

Proper monitoring is essential for a healthy aquaponic system. Key parameters to track include:

  • Water temperature (optimal: 68-78¬∞F)
  • pH level (optimal: 6.8-7.2)
  • Ammonia levels (should be <0.5 ppm)
  • Nitrite levels (should be <1 ppm)
Important: This monitor provides analysis based on water parameters. Regular testing and monitoring are essential. Sudden changes in parameters can harm fish and plants. Take immediate action if levels are outside safe ranges.

Ammonia is toxic to fish and should be kept at 0 ppm. Beneficial bacteria convert ammonia to nitrites, then to nitrates which feed plants. Maintain a stable bacterial colony for a healthy system.
