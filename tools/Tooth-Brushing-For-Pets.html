Pet Dental Care Information

Regular dental care is essential for your pet's overall health:

  • Prevents dental diseases and tooth loss
  • Reduces risk of heart, liver, and kidney problems
  • Improves breath and eating comfort
  • Prevents painful dental conditions
Important: This scheduler provides general dental care recommendations. Always use pet-specific toothpaste and brushes. Consult your veterinarian if you notice signs of dental problems like bad breath, yellow teeth, or difficulty eating.

Daily brushing is ideal for most pets. Start slowly with short sessions and gradually increase. Use pet-friendly toothpaste (never human toothpaste). Regular professional cleanings are also recommended.
