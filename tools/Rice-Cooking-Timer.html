Rice Cooking Tips

  • Use a 1:2 ratio of rice to water for most white rice varieties
  • Rinse rice before cooking to remove excess starch
  • Bring water to a boil before adding rice
  • Once boiling, reduce heat to low and cover the pot
  • Don't lift the lid while cooking to retain steam
  • Let rice rest for 5-10 minutes after cooking before fluffing
  • For fluffier rice, add a pinch of salt to the water
  • Brown rice needs more water and longer cooking time than white rice
