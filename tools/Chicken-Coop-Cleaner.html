Chicken Coop Cleaning Information

Regular coop cleaning is essential for maintaining healthy chickens and preventing disease. Proper cleaning schedules depend on:

  • Coop size and number of chickens
  • Seasonal conditions
  • Cleaning type (light, deep, disinfection)
  • Environmental factors
Important: This cleaner provides recommendations for coop maintenance. Always use appropriate protective equipment when cleaning. Ensure proper ventilation during cleaning. Replace bedding regularly to maintain hygiene.

Deep clean your coop at least once every 3-4 months. Remove all bedding, wash surfaces with appropriate disinfectant, and allow to dry completely before adding fresh bedding.
