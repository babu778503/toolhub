Diet Allergy Information

Identifying food allergies in pets is crucial for their health:

  • Common allergens include proteins like chicken, beef, and dairy
  • Grains like wheat and corn can also cause reactions
  • Symptoms include itching, digestive issues, and ear infections
  • Elimination diets help identify specific allergens
Important: This checker provides general allergen information. For persistent allergy symptoms, consult with a veterinarian. Do not rely solely on this tool for serious health issues.

Food allergies in pets require careful management. Start with a limited ingredient diet and gradually introduce new foods to identify triggers. Keep a food diary to track reactions.
