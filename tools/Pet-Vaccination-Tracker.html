Pet Vaccination Information

Regular vaccinations are essential for protecting your pet's health. Core vaccines are recommended for all pets, while non-core vaccines depend on lifestyle and risk factors:

  • Dogs: DHPP (Distemper, Hepatitis, Parvovirus, Parainfluenza), Rabies
  • Cats: FVRCP (Feline Viral Rhinotracheitis, Calicivirus, Panleukopenia), Rabies
  • Vaccination schedules vary by age, health, and local regulations
  • Booster shots maintain immunity over time
Important: This tracker provides general vaccination schedule recommendations. Always consult your veterinarian for a personalized vaccination plan based on your pet's health, lifestyle, and local requirements. Vaccination schedules may vary by region.

Puppies and kittens need multiple vaccines in their first year. Adult pets typically need boosters every 1-3 years depending on the vaccine. Keep vaccination records accessible for boarding, grooming, and travel requirements.
