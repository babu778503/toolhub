Herb Drying Safety Information

Proper herb drying is essential for preserving flavor and preventing mold. Optimal drying depends on:

  • Herb type and moisture content
  • Drying method used
  • Quantity of herbs
  • Available drying space
Important: This planner provides recommendations for optimal drying setup. Always ensure proper air circulation and avoid overcrowding. Herbs should be completely dry before storage to prevent mold.

For best results, harvest herbs before flowering when oil content is highest. Wash gently if needed, then pat dry. Hang in small bundles in a warm, dry, well-ventilated area away from direct sunlight.
