How to Measure

  1. Use a piece of string or paper to wrap around the base of your finger
  2. Mark the point where the ends meet
  3. Measure the length with a ruler
  4. Measure at the end of the day when fingers are warm
  5. Leave a little room for comfort (1-2mm extra)

Tip: If you're between sizes, round up to the next size for comfort.
