Macro Distribution Guide

Athletes

  • Higher carb intake (5-7g/kg body weight)
  • Adequate protein (1.6-2.2g/kg body weight)
  • Moderate fat (20-30% of total calories)

Weight Loss

  • Moderate carb restriction
  • Higher protein intake (30-35%)
  • Controlled fat intake

Vegetarian

  • Plant-based protein sources
  • Higher carb focus
  • Essential fatty acids from nuts/seeds

High Protein

  • Lean meats and fish
  • Low-fat dairy products
  • Egg whites and legumes
