Water Fountain Cleaning Information

Regular water fountain cleaning is essential for your pet's health:

  • Prevents bacterial growth and algae formation
  • Ensures fresh, clean water for your pet
  • Extends the life of your fountain
  • Prevents mineral buildup and clogs
Important: This cleaner provides general cleaning recommendations. Always follow manufacturer instructions for your specific fountain model. Replace filters as recommended. Never use harsh chemicals that could harm your pet.

Ceramic fountains require more frequent cleaning due to porous surfaces. Stainless steel is easier to clean and maintain. Plastic fountains may develop scratches that harbor bacteria. Glass fountains are easy to clean but fragile. Filtered water reduces cleaning frequency but filters need regular replacement.
