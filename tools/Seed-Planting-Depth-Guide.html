Planting Tips

General Rule
Plant seeds at a depth of 2-3 times their diameter for optimal germination.
Soil Temperature
Most seeds germinate best when soil temperature is between 65-75°F (18-24°C).
Soil Preparation
Ensure soil is loose, well-draining, and free of weeds before planting.
