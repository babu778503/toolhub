Jewelry Cleaning Tips

  • Clean rings every 1-2 months to maintain shine
  • Necklaces and bracelets should be cleaned monthly
  • Earrings need cleaning after each wear
  • Use mild soap and warm water for most jewelry
  • Store jewelry in a dry place to prevent tarnishing
