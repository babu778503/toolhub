Training Reward Information

Proper treat management during training is crucial for your pet's health:

  • Limit treats to 10% of daily caloric intake
  • Use small, high-value treats for better focus
  • Adjust meal portions when giving treats
  • Choose healthy, training-specific treats
Important: This calculator provides general treat recommendations. Adjust based on your pet's specific dietary needs and consult your veterinarian for pets with health conditions. Monitor your pet's weight and adjust treat amounts accordingly.

For dogs, use pea-sized treats for small breeds and marble-sized for large breeds. Cats prefer tiny treats. Break larger treats into smaller pieces to maintain training momentum without overfeeding.
