Coat Shedding Information

Understanding your pet's shedding patterns helps maintain coat health:

  • Double-coated breeds shed heavily twice yearly
  • Seasonal changes affect shedding cycles
  • Proper nutrition supports healthy coat
  • Regular grooming reduces loose fur
Important: This monitor provides general shedding information. Excessive shedding can indicate health issues. Consult your veterinarian if you notice bald patches, skin irritation, or sudden changes in shedding patterns.

Spring and fall are peak shedding seasons for most pets. During these times, increase brushing frequency to manage loose fur. Provide omega fatty acid supplements for coat health. Double-coated breeds require special attention during shedding seasons.
