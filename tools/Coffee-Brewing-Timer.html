Coffee Brewing Tips

  • Use a 1:15 to 1:17 coffee-to-water ratio for best results
  • Grind beans just before brewing for maximum freshness
  • Water temperature should be between 195-205°F (90-96°C)
  • Preheat your brewing equipment to maintain temperature
  • Store coffee beans in an airtight container away from light
  • Experiment with grind size and brew time to find your perfect cup
  • For pour over, use a circular motion for even saturation
  • Clean your equipment regularly for consistent taste
