Cat Litter Care Information

Proper litter box maintenance is crucial for your cat's health and household hygiene. General guidelines include:

  • Scoop daily (or twice daily for multiple cats)
  • Change clumping litter completely every 2-3 weeks
  • Change non-clumping litter weekly
  • Clean and disinfect the box during each change
Important: This reminder provides general litter change recommendations. Adjust based on your cat's preferences, health, and household conditions. Consult your veterinarian if your cat shows signs of litter box avoidance.

Clumping litter typically lasts 2-3 weeks with daily scooping. Non-clumping litter needs more frequent changes. Silica crystals can last 4-6 weeks. Always wash the box with mild soap when changing litter completely.
