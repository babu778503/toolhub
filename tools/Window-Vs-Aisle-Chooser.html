Window Seat Advantages

  • Scenic views during flight
  • Support for sleeping
  • Less disturbance from other passengers

Aisle Seat Advantages

  • Easy access to bathroom
  • Ability to stretch legs
  • More freedom to move

Seat Selection Tips:

  • Window seats are ideal for sleeping and scenic views
  • Aisle seats are better for passengers who need to move around
  • For long flights, consider your comfort preferences
  • Check the aircraft layout before selecting seats
  • Research airline seat maps online before booking
  • Consider exit rows for extra legroom (may have restrictions)
  • Bulkhead seats offer more legroom but no under-seat storage
