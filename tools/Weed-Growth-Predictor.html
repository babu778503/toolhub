Weed Prevention Tips

Mulching
Apply 2-4 inches of organic mulch to prevent weed seed germination.
Regular Maintenance
Remove weeds before they flower and set seed to prevent spread.
Proper Watering
Water deeply but less frequently to encourage deep root growth in desired plants.
Competition
Plant dense ground covers to outcompete weeds for space and nutrients.
