Collar Size Information

Finding the right collar size ensures your pet's comfort and safety:

  • Measure neck circumference snugly but not tightly
  • Add 1-2 inches for proper fit allowance
  • Consider fur thickness in measurements
  • Check fit regularly as pets grow
Important: This finder provides size recommendations only. Always test actual collars on your pet. A properly fitted collar should allow two fingers between the collar and neck. Too tight causes choking risk; too loose may slip off.

Martingale collars work well for dogs with narrow heads. Breakaway collars are ideal for cats. Flat collars suit most pets. Puppies and kittens grow quickly, requiring frequent size checks. Measure during the day when not feeding to avoid neck swelling.
