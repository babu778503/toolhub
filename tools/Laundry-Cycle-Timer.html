Laundry Tips

  • Sort clothes by color and fabric type before washing
  • Don't overload the washing machine for best results
  • Use cold water for dark colors to prevent bleeding
  • Pre-treat stains before washing for better removal
  • Leave space for clothes to move freely in the drum
  • Check pockets for items before washing
  • Use the appropriate amount of detergent for load size
