Canning Safety Information

Proper canning is essential for food safety. Processing times vary based on:

  • Food type (acidic vs. low-acid)
  • Jar size
  • Altitude above sea level
  • Processing method used
Important: This calculator provides estimates only. Always follow USDA guidelines and specific recipes for safe canning practices. Improper canning can lead to foodborne illness.

For low-acid foods (vegetables, meat, poultry, seafood), a pressure canner is required. Water bath canning is only safe for high-acid foods like fruits and pickled vegetables.
