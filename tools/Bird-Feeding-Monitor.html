Bird Feeding Information

Proper bird feeding supports local wildlife and provides enjoyable wildlife viewing. Best practices include:

  • Consistent feeding schedules
  • Seasonal adjustments to food types
  • Regular feeder cleaning
  • Providing fresh water
Important: This monitor provides general feeding recommendations. Adjust based on local bird populations and seasonal availability. Clean feeders regularly to prevent disease transmission. Consult local birding guides for species-specific needs.

In winter, birds need high-energy foods like black oil sunflower seeds. During breeding season, provide protein-rich options like mealworms. Clean feeders monthly with a 10% bleach solution. Position feeders safely from predators.
