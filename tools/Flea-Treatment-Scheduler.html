Flea Treatment Information

Regular flea prevention is essential for pet health and comfort:

  • Prevents flea infestations in your home
  • Reduces risk of flea-borne diseases
  • Prevents allergic reactions in sensitive pets
  • Protects other pets in the household
Important: This scheduler provides general treatment recommendations. Always follow product instructions and consult your veterinarian before starting any flea prevention program. Some treatments are not suitable for young pets or pregnant animals.

Most flea treatments need to be applied monthly for optimal protection. Start prevention before fleas become active in your area (typically spring). Treat all pets in the household simultaneously for best results.
