Pet Camera Information

Remote pet monitoring provides peace of mind when you're away from home:

  • Check on your pet's wellbeing anytime
  • Monitor for signs of distress or illness
  • See how your pet interacts with others
  • Receive alerts for unusual activity
Important: This viewer simulates a pet camera interface. For actual pet monitoring, purchase a compatible camera system. Ensure your camera is positioned safely away from your pet's reach. Respect privacy laws when placing cameras in shared spaces.

When choosing a pet camera, consider WiFi connectivity, night vision capabilities, and motion detection sensitivity. Battery-powered cameras offer flexibility in placement. Cloud storage provides remote access but may incur subscription fees. Local storage is more affordable but requires physical access to retrieve footage.
