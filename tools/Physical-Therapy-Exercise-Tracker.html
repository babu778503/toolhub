  • Consistency: Perform exercises as prescribed by your therapist
  • Form: Focus on proper technique over quantity
  • Progression: Gradually increase intensity as tolerated
  • Pain Monitoring: Stop if pain increases significantly
  • Communication: Report concerns to your therapist

Therapy Resources

Common Physical Therapy Exercises

Range of Motion: Gentle movements to maintain joint flexibility

Strengthening: Progressive resistance exercises to rebuild muscle strength

Balance Training: Exercises to improve stability and coordination

Functional Training: Activities that mimic daily tasks

Manual Therapy: Hands-on techniques by the therapist

Recovery Timeline Expectations

  • Week 1-2: Focus on pain reduction and gentle movement
  • Week 3-4: Begin strengthening exercises
  • Week 5-8: Progress to functional activities
  • Week 9+: Return to full activity as tolerated

Home Exercise Tips

  • Create a dedicated exercise space in your home
  • Set realistic daily goals and track progress
  • Warm up before and cool down after exercises
  • Use proper equipment (mats, resistance bands, etc.)
  • Stay consistent with your routine
  • Listen to your body and rest when needed
