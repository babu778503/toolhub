General Carpet Cleaning Steps

  1. Blot (don't rub) the stain with paper towels to absorb as much liquid as possible
  2. Apply the recommended cleaning solution according to the instructions below
  3. Allow the solution to sit for the recommended time
  4. Rinse with clean water and blot dry
  5. Use a fan or allow to air dry completely

Recommended Cleaning Solutions

  • For urine: Enzyme cleaner (PetSafe Stain & Odor Remover)
  • For feces: Mild detergent solution
  • For vomit: Baking soda paste + vinegar solution
  • For drool: Mild soap solution

Helpful Tips

  • Always test cleaning solutions on a hidden area first
  • Work from the outside of the stain toward the center
  • Never use ammonia-based cleaners on pet stains
  • For stubborn odors, consider using an enzyme-based cleaner
  • Regular vacuuming prevents buildup of pet hair and dander

Pet Safety

Keep pets away from treated areas until completely dry. Some cleaning solutions may be harmful if ingested.

Prevention

Regular vacuuming and immediate attention to accidents can prevent permanent stains and odors.

Professional Help

For large or persistent stains, consider professional carpet cleaning services.
