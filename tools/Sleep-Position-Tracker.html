Sleep Position Information

Tracking your pet's sleep positions can reveal important health insights:

  • Curling indicates comfort and security
  • Stretching shows relaxation and trust
  • Belly up position indicates complete trust
  • Changes in sleep positions may signal health issues
Important: This tracker provides general sleep position analysis. Persistent changes in sleep patterns or positions may indicate health issues. Consult your veterinarian if you notice significant changes or concerning behaviors.

Dogs that sleep on their backs feel completely safe in their environment. Cats that curl tightly are conserving body heat. Birds that tuck their heads under wings are in deep sleep. Rabbits that stretch out are feeling secure and relaxed. Monitor sleep positions along with overall behavior for comprehensive health assessment.
