Whisker Fatigue Information

Whisker fatigue is a real condition that affects cats:

  • Caused by repeated contact with narrow bowls
  • Results in discomfort and stress
  • Can lead to reduced appetite
  • Prevented with proper bowl selection
Important: This preventer provides recommendations for whisker-friendly bowls. If your cat shows signs of whisker fatigue (pawing food out, reluctance to eat, head tilting), consult your veterinarian. Always use shallow, wide bowls for cats.

Cats' whiskers are highly sensitive tactile organs that help them navigate their environment. When whiskers repeatedly touch the sides of narrow bowls, it can cause discomfort similar to wearing tight shoes all day. Shallow, wide bowls prevent this contact and reduce stress.
