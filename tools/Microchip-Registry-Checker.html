Microchip Registry Information

Keeping your pet's microchip registry up to date is crucial for recovery:

  • Verify registration annually
  • Update contact information after moves
  • Transfer ownership when pet changes homes
  • Check multiple registries if unsure of provider
Important: This checker verifies registry information only. For actual updates, contact the specific registry directly. Microchips don't track location - they store identification numbers linked to your contact information in databases.

Most microchips can be registered with multiple registries for redundancy. When adopting a pet, always verify the microchip is registered to you and not the previous owner. Keep registration information in a safe place and update it whenever you move or change phone numbers.
