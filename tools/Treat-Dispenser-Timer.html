Treat Dispenser Information

Proper treat dispensing timing is essential for effective pet training:

  • Consistent timing reinforces desired behaviors
  • Appropriate intervals prevent overfeeding
  • Varied timing helps generalize learning
  • Correct treat size matches training intensity
Important: This timer helps manage treat dispensing for training. Always use healthy, appropriately sized treats. Monitor your pet for signs of overexcitement or distress. Adjust intervals based on your pet's attention span and energy level.

For basic commands, dispense treats immediately after correct responses. For complex behaviors, use a variable ratio schedule (sometimes after 1 response, sometimes after 3-5). Small treats are ideal for high-frequency training. Larger treats work well for occasional rewards.
