Recycling Tips

Best Practices for Recycling

  • Empty and rinse containers before recycling
  • Remove caps and lids unless specified otherwise
  • Flatten cardboard boxes to save space
  • Check local guidelines for specific items
  • Separate materials by type when possible
  • Know what cannot be recycled in your area
