Hydroponic Nutrient Information

Proper nutrient mixing is essential for healthy plant growth in hydroponic systems. Optimal nutrient solutions depend on:

  • Plant type and species
  • Growth stage (seedling, vegetative, flowering)
  • Water volume and target PPM
  • Nutrient base formula
Important: This mixer provides recommendations for nutrient solutions. Always measure PPM with a meter and adjust pH to 5.5-6.5 for optimal nutrient uptake. Monitor plants for signs of nutrient deficiency or toxicity.

Leafy greens typically require lower PPM during early growth (400-600), while fruiting plants need higher PPM during flowering (1000-1200). Change nutrient solution every 7-10 days for best results.
