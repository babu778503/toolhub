How to Measure

  1. Stand upright with your feet together
  2. Place the tape measure around your natural waistline
  3. Keep the tape snug but not tight
  4. Measure at the point where you normally wear your belt
  5. Take the measurement in inches or centimeters

Tip: If you're between sizes, round up to the next even number for comfort.
