Animal Weight Tracking Information

Regular weight monitoring is essential for animal health and farm profitability. Proper weight management helps:

  • Monitor animal growth and development
  • Optimize feeding programs
  • Detect health issues early
  • Make informed marketing decisions
Important: This tracker provides estimates for weight gain plans. Actual weight gain varies based on animal genetics, health, nutrition, and environmental conditions. Consult with a veterinarian for precise health and nutrition recommendations.

Cattle typically gain 2-4 lbs per day during optimal growth phases. Pigs can gain 1.5-2.5 lbs per day. Monitor animals regularly and adjust feeding programs based on performance and health indicators.
