Grow Light Information

Proper lighting is essential for healthy plant growth indoors. Optimal light schedules vary based on:

  • Plant type and species
  • Growth stage (seedling, vegetative, flowering)
  • Light intensity and spectrum
  • Distance from light source
Important: This timer provides recommendations for optimal light schedules. Monitor your plants for signs of light stress (burning, stretching) and adjust accordingly. Different plants have varying light requirements.

Most seedlings require 14-16 hours of light daily. Vegetative plants typically need 18 hours of light, while flowering plants need 12 hours of light and 12 hours of darkness to trigger blooming.
