Drying Safety Information

Proper fruit dehydration is essential for food safety and quality. Optimal drying times vary based on:

  • Fruit type and ripeness
  • Preparation method (sliced, whole, etc.)
  • Dehydrator temperature
  • Humidity levels
Important: This timer provides estimates only. Always follow proper dehydration practices. Fruits should be completely dry with no visible moisture. Store in airtight containers after cooling completely.

For best results, slice fruits uniformly, maintain consistent temperature, and rotate trays periodically for even drying. Properly dried fruits can be stored for 6-12 months.
