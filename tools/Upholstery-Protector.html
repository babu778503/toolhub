General Protection Steps

  1. Vacuum the upholstery thoroughly to remove dust and debris
  2. Test the protector on a hidden area to ensure compatibility
  3. Apply the protector evenly according to the product instructions
  4. Allow the protector to dry completely before using the furniture
  5. Repeat the application as needed based on usage level

Recommended Protectors

  • For cotton: Scotchgard Fabric & Upholstery Protector
  • For leather: Leather Honey Conditioner
  • For microfiber: Guardsman Fabric & Leather Protector
  • For suede: Suede Magic Cleaner
  • For velvet: Folex Carpet Spot Remover

Helpful Tips

  • Always vacuum before applying any protector
  • Ensure the fabric is completely dry before application
  • Work in a well-ventilated area
  • Follow manufacturer instructions for specific products
  • Reapply protectors after cleaning or annually

Fabric Care

Each fabric type requires specific care. Always check the manufacturer's care instructions before applying any product.

Prevention

Regular vacuuming and immediate attention to spills can prevent permanent damage and extend the life of your upholstery.

Professional Help

For valuable or delicate upholstery, consider professional treatment services.
