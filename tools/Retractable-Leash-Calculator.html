Leash Safety Tips

  • Always maintain visual contact with your dog
  • Keep leash length appropriate for the environment
  • Avoid retractable leashes in crowded areas
  • Check leash for wear and tear regularly
  • Train your dog to walk properly on a leash
  • Be aware of local leash laws and regulations
