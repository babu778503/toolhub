Soil pH Information

Soil pH affects nutrient availability. Most plants prefer slightly acidic to neutral soil (pH 6.0-7.0). Acidic soil (pH < 7) may require lime to raise pH. Alkaline soil (pH > 7) may benefit from sulfur or organic matter to lower pH.
