Human to Pet Age Conversion Information

Understanding the relationship between human and pet ages helps with care decisions:

  • Pets age faster in their early years compared to humans
  • Small dogs tend to live longer than large dogs
  • Cats age differently than dogs after the first two years
  • Senior pets have different care requirements
Important: This calculator provides estimates based on general aging patterns. Individual pets may age differently. Consult your veterinarian for specific health and care recommendations based on your pet's age.

For dogs: The first year is equivalent to 15 human years, the second year to 24 human years. After that, each dog year is approximately 4-5 human years depending on size. For cats: The first year is equivalent to 15 human years, the second to 24, and each subsequent year is about 4 human years.
