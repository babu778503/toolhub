Mood Types & Meanings

😊 Happy
Feeling joyful, content, or satisfied. Often accompanied by positive energy and optimism.
😢 Sad
Feeling down, disappointed, or melancholic. A natural response to loss or difficult situations.
😰 Anxious
Feeling worried, nervous, or uneasy about future events or uncertainties.
😠 Angry
Feeling irritated, frustrated, or upset about something that has happened.
😌 Calm
Feeling peaceful, relaxed, and at ease with yourself and your surroundings.
🤩 Excited
Feeling enthusiastic, eager, and full of positive anticipation.

Mental Wellness Tips

Practice Mindfulness
Spend 5-10 minutes daily focusing on your breath or observing your thoughts without judgment.
Stay Connected
Maintain social connections with friends and family. Reach out when you need support.
Physical Activity
Regular exercise releases endorphins which naturally boost your mood and reduce stress.
Sleep Hygiene
Maintain consistent sleep schedules and create a calming bedtime routine for better rest.
Journaling
Write down your thoughts and feelings to process emotions and identify patterns.
Seek Professional Help
If you're struggling with persistent negative emotions, consider talking to a mental health professional.
Understanding Your Emotions

All emotions are valid: There's no "right" or "wrong" way to feel. Emotions are natural responses to your experiences.

Pattern recognition: Tracking your moods over time can help identify triggers and patterns in your emotional well-being.
