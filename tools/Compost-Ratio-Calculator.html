Compost Ratio Information

The ideal carbon to nitrogen (C:N) ratio for composting is between 25:1 and 30:1. This ratio provides the right balance of materials for efficient decomposition. Carbon-rich materials (browns) provide energy for decomposers, while nitrogen-rich materials (greens) provide protein for microorganisms.
