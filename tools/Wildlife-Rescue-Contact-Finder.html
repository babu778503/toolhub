Wildlife Rescue Information

Knowing how to contact wildlife rescues can save animal lives:

  • Never attempt to handle wild animals without training
  • Keep pets and children away from injured wildlife
  • Provide location details and animal condition accurately
  • Follow rescuer instructions carefully
Important: This finder provides general contact information. For immediate emergencies, call local emergency services (911 in US/Canada). Always verify contact information as it may change. Do not feed or give water to injured wildlife.

If you find injured wildlife, keep a safe distance and observe from afar until help arrives. Place the animal in a secure box with air holes if necessary, but do not attempt handling. Many areas have specific hotlines for different wildlife types - birds, mammals, marine life, etc.
