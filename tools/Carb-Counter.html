Health Benefits

Cardiovascular Health

Complex carbs help maintain heart health and reduce cholesterol levels.

Weight Management

Fiber-rich carbs promote satiety and help control appetite.

Brain Function

Carb Intake Guidelines

Balanced Diet

Low Carb

50-100g per day
