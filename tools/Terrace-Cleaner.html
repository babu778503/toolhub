General Terrace Cleaning Steps

  1. Remove all furniture, planters, and debris from the terrace
  2. Sweep the surface to remove loose dirt and leaves
  3. Apply the recommended cleaning solution according to the instructions below
  4. Allow the solution to sit for the recommended time
  5. Rinse thoroughly with clean water
  6. Allow to air dry completely before replacing furniture

Recommended Cleaning Solutions

  • For concrete: Concrete cleaner or diluted bleach solution
  • For stone: Stone-specific cleaner or mild detergent
  • For wood: Wood deck cleaner or oxalic acid solution
  • For tile: Tile and grout cleaner
  • For pavers: Paver-specific cleaner

Helpful Tips

  • Always test cleaning solutions on a small area first
  • Work in sections to ensure even cleaning
  • Use a pressure washer for heavily soiled areas (with caution)
  • Seal porous surfaces after cleaning for protection
  • Wear protective gear when using cleaning chemicals

Safety First

Always wear gloves and eye protection when using cleaning chemicals. Ensure good ventilation in enclosed areas.

Prevention

Regular sweeping and prompt attention to spills can prevent stubborn stains from forming.

Professional Help

For large or heavily stained terraces, consider professional cleaning services.
