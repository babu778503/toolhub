Veterinary Care Information

Regular veterinary visits are essential for maintaining your pet's health. Recommended visit frequencies vary based on:

  • Pet age (puppies/kittens need more frequent visits)
  • Pet breed and health status
  • Vaccination requirements
  • Preventive care needs
Important: This scheduler provides recommendations for routine veterinary visits. Always consult your veterinarian for specific health concerns or emergencies. Vaccination schedules vary by region and pet lifestyle.

Adult pets typically need annual checkups. Puppies and kittens require more frequent visits for initial vaccinations. Senior pets (7+ years) may benefit from bi-annual visits to monitor age-related health changes.
