Life Expectancy Information

Life expectancy varies significantly by pet type and breed:

  • Dogs: Small breeds typically live longer than large breeds
  • Cats: Indoor cats generally live longer than outdoor cats
  • Proper nutrition and healthcare extend lifespan
  • Regular exercise contributes to longevity
Important: This estimator provides general life expectancy estimates based on breed and health factors. Individual pets may live longer or shorter lives. Consult your veterinarian for specific health recommendations.

Small dogs (under 20 lbs) typically live 12-16 years, while large dogs (over 50 lbs) often live 8-12 years. Cats generally live 12-18 years. Proper care, nutrition, and regular veterinary checkups can significantly impact your pet's lifespan.
