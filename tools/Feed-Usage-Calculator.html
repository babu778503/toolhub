Feed Usage Information

Proper feed management is essential for animal health and farm profitability. Feed requirements depend on:

  • Animal type and breed
  • Animal size and age
  • Production stage (growth, laying, lactation)
  • Seasonal factors
Important: This calculator provides estimates for feed requirements. Actual consumption may vary based on animal health, environmental conditions, and feed quality. Consult with a veterinarian or animal nutritionist for precise feeding recommendations.

Chickens typically consume 0.25-0.5 lbs of feed per day. Cattle require 2-4% of their body weight daily. Adjust feed amounts based on animal condition and production goals. Store feed in dry, pest-free conditions.
