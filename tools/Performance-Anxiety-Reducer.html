4-7-8 Breathing

1. Inhale quietly through your nose for 4 counts

2. Hold your breath for 7 counts

3. Exhale completely through your mouth for 8 counts

Repeat 4 times to calm your nervous system

Box Breathing

1. Inhale for 4 counts

2. Hold for 4 counts

3. Exhale for 4 counts

4. Hold for 4 counts

Repeat the cycle to create a calming rhythm

Progressive Muscle Relaxation

1. Tense your muscles for 5 seconds

2. Release and relax for 10 seconds

3. Move to the next muscle group

Start from your toes and work up to your head

Guided Visualization

Engage all your senses - see, hear, smell, touch

Picture yourself performing confidently

Visualize success and positive outcomes
