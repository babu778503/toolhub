Freezing Safety Information

Proper freezing is essential for maintaining food quality and safety. Optimal freezing times vary based on:

  • Food type (vegetables, fruits, meat, etc.)
  • Packaging method
  • Freezer temperature
  • Storage duration
Important: This tracker provides recommendations for optimal quality. Food stored at 0°F is safe indefinitely, but quality deteriorates over time. Always follow proper food handling practices.

For best results, maintain a consistent freezer temperature of 0°F (-18°C) or below. Label items with the freezing date and use proper packaging to prevent freezer burn.
