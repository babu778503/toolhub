Signs of Ripeness

Firm but slightly soft to gentle pressure
Bright, consistent color
Sweet aroma at the stem
Smooth skin without wrinkles

Signs of Overripeness

Soft spots or mushy areas
Dull or wrinkled skin
Sour or fermented smell
Brown spots on skin

Storage Tips

Store in refrigerator to slow ripening
Keep away from other fruits to prevent ethylene exposure
Consume within 1-2 weeks for best quality
