Fertilizer NPK Ratio Information

The three numbers on fertilizer represent Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), and Potassium (K). Different ratios are suitable for different lawn needs. 10-10-10 is balanced, 16-4-8 is for new lawns, 20-5-10 is for established lawns, 24-3-6 is for quick greening.
