Dog Backpack Information

Proper backpack planning ensures safe and enjoyable hiking experiences with your dog:

  • Choose appropriate backpack size for your dog
  • Gradually build up hiking endurance
  • Pack essentials for both you and your dog
  • Monitor your dog for signs of fatigue
Important: This planner provides general backpack recommendations. Always consult your veterinarian before starting hiking with your dog. Start with short, easy hikes and gradually increase difficulty. Never force your dog to continue if they show signs of distress.

Dogs should carry no more than 10-15% of their body weight. Small dogs may need specially designed packs. Ensure proper fit with adjustable straps. Include fresh water, waste bags, and a first aid kit. Check paws regularly for injuries during hikes.
