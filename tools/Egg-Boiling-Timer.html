Egg Boiling Tips

  • Start with cold water and bring to a boil with eggs in the pot
  • Use eggs at room temperature for more even cooking
  • Add a pinch of salt to the water to prevent cracking
  • For easy peeling, use eggs that are at least a week old
  • Transfer boiled eggs to ice water immediately to stop cooking
  • Roll boiled eggs gently on the counter to crack the shell all over for easier peeling
