Sunrise Sunset Guidelines:

  • Sunrise is the time when the upper limb of the sun appears on the horizon
  • Sunset is the time when the upper limb of the sun disappears below the horizon
  • Golden hour occurs during the first and last hour of sunlight
  • Blue hour occurs just before sunrise and after sunset
  • Daylight hours vary by season and latitude
  • Daylight saving time affects sunrise and sunset times
