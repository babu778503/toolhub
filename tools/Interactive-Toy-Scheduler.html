Interactive Toy Scheduling Information

Regular interactive playtime is essential for your pet's mental and physical health:

  • Prevents boredom and destructive behavior
  • Provides mental stimulation and enrichment
  • Strengthens the bond between pet and owner
  • Helps maintain physical fitness
Important: This scheduler provides general playtime recommendations. Adjust based on your pet's individual needs and preferences. Always supervise pets during playtime with interactive toys. Remove damaged toys immediately.

Puppies and kittens need frequent short play sessions. Adult pets benefit from structured playtime. Senior pets may prefer gentler interactive toys. High-energy dogs require more frequent and intense play sessions. Puzzle toys are excellent for mental stimulation.
