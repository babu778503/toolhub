Pet Exercise Information

Regular exercise is essential for your pet's physical and mental health:

  • Maintains healthy weight and muscle tone
  • Reduces behavioral problems and anxiety
  • Improves cardiovascular health
  • Strengthens bond between pet and owner
Important: This planner provides general exercise recommendations. Always consult your veterinarian before starting a new exercise program, especially for senior pets or those with health conditions. Adjust activities based on your pet's individual needs and abilities.

Dogs typically need 30-120 minutes of exercise daily depending on breed and energy level. Cats benefit from multiple short play sessions throughout the day. Small pets like rabbits need supervised exercise time outside their enclosure daily.
