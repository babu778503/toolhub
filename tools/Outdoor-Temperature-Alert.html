Temperature Safety Guidelines

• Safe outdoor temperature: 15-25°C (59-77°F)
• Caution zone: 25-32°C (77-90°F)
• Danger zone: Above 32°C (90°F)
• Pets with thick coats or health issues are more vulnerable
• Humidity increases heat stress significantly
• Never leave pets in hot cars
