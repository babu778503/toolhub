Different grass types require different seeding rates per square foot. Fescue: 6-8 lbs/1000 sq ft, Bluegrass: 2-3 lbs/1000 sq ft, Ryegrass: 8-10 lbs/1000 sq ft, Zoysia: 1-2 lbs/1000 sq ft.
