Eye Care Information

Regular eye care is essential for your pet's health and comfort:

  • Prevents infections and irritations
  • Removes discharge and debris
  • Improves vision and comfort
  • Allows early detection of problems
Important: This tracker provides general eye care recommendations. Consult your veterinarian immediately if you notice persistent discharge, redness, swelling, cloudiness, or if your pet is pawing at their eyes. Never use human eye drops on pets.

Some pets naturally produce more eye discharge, especially those with flat faces (brachycephalic breeds) or long hair around their eyes. Use a clean, damp cloth or pet-safe wipes for cleaning. Wipe from the inner corner outward to avoid spreading contaminants.
