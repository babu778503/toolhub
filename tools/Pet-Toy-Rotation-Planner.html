Pet Toy Rotation Information

Toy rotation is an effective way to maintain your pet's interest and provide mental stimulation:

  • Keeps pets engaged with toys longer
  • Prevents destructive behavior
  • Provides mental stimulation
  • Extends toy life
Important: This planner provides general toy rotation recommendations. Adjust based on your pet's individual preferences and behavior. Always supervise pets with new toys and remove damaged items immediately.

For dogs, rotate between different types (squeaky, rope, puzzle, plush). For cats, alternate between interactive and solo toys. Birds enjoy foraging and puzzle toys. Small pets like hamsters benefit from tunnels and wheels.
