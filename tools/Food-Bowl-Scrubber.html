Eco-Friendly Tips

  • Reuse water from pet bowls to water plants

How to Use:

  • Adjust scrubbing intensity and duration using the sliders
  • Select your bowl type for optimized cleaning
  • Click and drag the scrubber across the bowl to clean it
  • Watch the cleanliness percentage increase as you scrub
  • Press "Start Scrubbing" for automated cleaning
  • Press "Reset Bowl" to start over with a dirty bowl
  • Press "Clear Data" to reset all settings to default
  • Press "Save Settings" to store your preferences
