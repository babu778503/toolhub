Developmental Guidelines

0-3 Months

  • Lifts head while lying on stomach
  • Follows moving objects with eyes
  • Responds to sounds
  • Smiles at people

4-6 Months

  • Rolls over in both directions
  • Begins to sit with support
  • Babbles and makes sounds
  • Reaches for toys

7-9 Months

  • Sits without support
  • Crawls forward on belly
  • Begins to stand
  • Uses thumb and forefinger

10-12 Months

  • Stands alone
  • Takes first steps
  • Says first words
  • Uses gestures
