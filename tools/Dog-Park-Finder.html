Dog Park Information

Regular visits to dog parks provide essential benefits for your pet:

  • Socialization with other dogs
  • Physical exercise and mental stimulation
  • Opportunity to practice recall commands
  • Safe off-leash environment
Important: Always supervise your dog at parks. Ensure vaccinations are current. Bring water and waste bags. Follow park rules regarding aggressive dogs and cleanup requirements.

Before visiting a new park, observe the environment and other dogs. Bring familiar toys and treats. Visit during off-peak hours initially to reduce overwhelm. Small dogs may benefit from separate small dog areas.
