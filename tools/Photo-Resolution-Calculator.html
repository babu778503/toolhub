Photo Resolution Calculator

How to Use This Calculator

Enter the physical dimensions of your photo (width and height) and the resolution (DPI) to calculate the total pixel dimensions. For example, if you're printing a 4x6 inch photo at 300 DPI, enter 4 for width, 6 for height, and 300 for DPI.

Example:

For a 8x10 inch print at 300 DPI:
Width: 8 inches
Height: 10 inches
DPI: 300
Result: 2400 x 3000 pixels
