Stray Animal Reporting Information

Reporting stray animals helps protect both animals and community members:

  • Connects animals with rescue organizations
  • Prevents potential accidents or conflicts
  • Provides medical care for injured animals
  • Reduces overpopulation through spay/neuter programs
Important: This reporter submits information to local databases. For immediate dangers, contact emergency services (911 in US/Canada). Never attempt to capture wild or aggressive animals yourself. Provide accurate location details for prompt response.

When reporting, include detailed descriptions and any distinguishing features. Photos (if possible) greatly help identification efforts. Many areas have specific hotlines for different animal types. Check if the animal has identification tags or a microchip before reporting.
