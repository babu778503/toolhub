Fish Tank Water Change Information

Regular water changes are essential for maintaining a healthy aquarium environment. Proper water change practices help:

  • Remove harmful nitrates and phosphates
  • Replace essential minerals
  • Maintain stable water parameters
  • Prevent algae growth
Important: This calculator provides general water change recommendations. Adjust based on your specific fish species, tank setup, and water parameters. Always use a water conditioner for tap water changes.

For new tanks (under 4 weeks), avoid water changes to allow beneficial bacteria to establish. After cycling, change 10-25% of water weekly. Saltwater tanks may require more frequent changes. Monitor water parameters regularly.
