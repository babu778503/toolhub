Travel Carrier Information

Proper carrier sizing ensures safe and comfortable travel for your pet:

  • Pet must stand, turn around, and lie down naturally
  • Carrier must meet airline size restrictions
  • Ventilation holes should be adequately sized
  • Secure locking mechanisms are essential
Important: This checker provides general carrier size recommendations. Always verify specific airline requirements before traveling. Measurements should be taken with pet standing normally. Contact airlines directly for exact policies and restrictions.

Most airlines require carriers to fit under the seat in front of you. Maximum dimensions typically range from 17-19 inches long, 10-12 inches wide, and 9-10 inches high. Soft-sided carriers are generally preferred for cabin travel as they compress for tight spaces.
