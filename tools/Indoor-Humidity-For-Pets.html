Humidity Guidelines for Pets

• Optimal humidity for most pets: 40-60%
• Low humidity: Below 30% (can cause dry skin, respiratory issues)
• High humidity: Above 70% (can promote bacteria/mold growth)
• Monitor humidity levels daily during extreme weather
• Use a hygrometer to measure accurately
