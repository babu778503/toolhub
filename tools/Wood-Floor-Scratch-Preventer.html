General Prevention Steps

  1. Regularly clean your wood floors to remove dirt and debris that can cause scratches
  2. Use furniture pads under all furniture legs to prevent indentation
  3. Keep pet nails trimmed to reduce scratching
  4. Use area rugs in high-traffic areas
  5. Place doormats at entrances to trap dirt
  6. Use felt pads on chair and table legs

Recommended Prevention Products

  • Furniture pads: Felt or rubber pads for furniture legs
  • Chair glides: For chairs that move frequently
  • Area rugs: To protect high-traffic areas
  • Door mats: To trap dirt at entrances
  • Microfiber cloths: For regular cleaning

Helpful Tips

  • Trim pet nails regularly to minimize scratching
  • Use washable area rugs in areas where pets spend time
  • Place furniture pads under all furniture legs
  • Use felt pads on chair and table legs
  • Keep pet food and water dishes in areas with non-wood flooring

Floor Care

Each floor type requires specific care. Hardwood needs different products than laminate or engineered floors.

Prevention

Regular maintenance and preventive measures are more effective than trying to fix scratches after they occur.
