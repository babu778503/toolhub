Pet Portion Information

Proper portion control is essential for your pet's health:

  • Overfeeding can lead to obesity and health issues
  • Underfeeding can cause malnutrition
  • Adjust portions based on activity level and age
  • Monitor your pet's weight regularly
Important: This calculator provides general portion recommendations. Adjust based on your pet's specific needs and consult your veterinarian for personalized feeding plans. Individual pets may have different caloric requirements.

For dogs: Puppies need more frequent meals (3-4 times daily) while adults typically eat twice daily. For cats: Most cats do well with 2-3 meals per day. Always provide fresh water with meals.
