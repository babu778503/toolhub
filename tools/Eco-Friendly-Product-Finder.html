Eco-Friendly Tips

How to Choose Eco-Friendly Products

  • Look for certifications like USDA Organic or Energy Star
  • Choose products with minimal packaging
  • Select items made from recycled materials
  • Opt for reusable over disposable products
  • Consider the product's lifecycle and disposal
  • Research the company's environmental practices
