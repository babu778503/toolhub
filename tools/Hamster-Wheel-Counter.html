Hamster Exercise Information

Regular exercise is crucial for hamster health and wellbeing. Wheel running provides:

  • Cardiovascular fitness
  • Mental stimulation
  • Weight management
  • Stress relief
Important: This counter provides estimates for hamster exercise. Actual activity varies by individual hamster. Monitor your pet for signs of over-exercising or injury. Ensure wheel is appropriately sized and safe.

Hamsters can run 5-8 miles per night in the wild. Provide a solid-surface wheel to prevent injury. Dwarf hamsters need smaller wheels (6-8 inches), while Syrians need larger wheels (8-12 inches). Clean wheels regularly to maintain hygiene.
