Dog Walking Information

Regular walks are essential for your dog's physical and mental health. Exercise needs vary based on:

  • Dog size and breed
  • Age and energy level
  • Health conditions
  • Weather and environment
Important: This schedule provides general exercise recommendations. Always consider your dog's individual needs and consult your veterinarian for specific health concerns. Adjust walks based on weather and your dog's comfort level.

Small dogs typically need 30-60 minutes of exercise daily. Large breeds may require 1-2 hours. Puppies need frequent, short walks while seniors may prefer gentler, longer sessions. Always bring water and clean-up bags.
