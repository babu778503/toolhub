Humidity Tips

  • Ideal indoor humidity levels are between 40-60%
  • Low humidity can cause dry skin and respiratory issues
  • High humidity promotes mold growth and dust mites
  • Use humidifiers in winter and dehumidifiers in summer
  • Plants naturally increase indoor humidity levels
  • Ventilate bathrooms and kitchens to reduce moisture
  • Monitor humidity levels especially during seasonal changes
