Grooming Appointment Information

Regular grooming is essential for your pet's health and wellbeing:

  • Improves coat health and reduces shedding
  • Helps identify skin issues early
  • Prevents matting and tangles
  • Maintains hygiene and cleanliness
Important: This reminder provides scheduling suggestions based on your inputs. Adjust based on your pet's specific needs and consult your veterinarian for grooming recommendations. Some pets may require more frequent grooming.

Most dogs need grooming every 4-8 weeks depending on coat type. Cats typically need less frequent grooming but long-haired breeds require regular brushing. Professional grooming is recommended for complex cuts or if you're not comfortable grooming at home.
