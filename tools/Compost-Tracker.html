Composting Tips

Best Practices for Successful Composting

  • Maintain a balance of "green" (nitrogen-rich) and "brown" (carbon-rich) materials
  • Chop materials into smaller pieces for faster decomposition
  • Turn your pile regularly to aerate it
  • Keep moisture levels similar to a wrung-out sponge
  • Ensure your pile reaches 140-160°F for effective decomposition
  • Avoid adding meat, dairy, or pet waste to your compost

How to Use This Compost Tracker

  • Record each addition to your compost pile with the date and material type
  • Track your total composted waste over time
  • Monitor your composting habits and adjust as needed
  • Use the statistics to understand your composting impact
  • Refer to composting tips for best practices
