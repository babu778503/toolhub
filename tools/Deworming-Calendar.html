Deworming Information

Regular deworming is essential for pet health and safety:

  • Prevents internal parasite infestations
  • Protects family members from zoonotic diseases
  • Improves pet's overall health and energy
  • Reduces risk of secondary infections
Important: This calendar provides general deworming recommendations. Always consult your veterinarian before starting any deworming program. Follow product instructions carefully and monitor your pet for adverse reactions.

Puppies and kittens should be dewormed every 2 weeks until 12 weeks old, then monthly until 6 months. Adult pets typically need deworming every 3-6 months. Pregnant animals require special consideration.
