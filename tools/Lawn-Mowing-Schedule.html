Lawn Mowing Tips

Mowing Height
Never cut more than 1/3 of the grass blade height in a single mowing.
Mowing Frequency
Mow more frequently during growing season, less during dormant periods.
Sharp Blades
Keep mower blades sharp for clean cuts that heal quickly.
Weather Considerations
Avoid mowing during extreme heat or when grass is wet.
