Portion Tips

  • When scaling up, increase seasonings by 1.25x instead of the full ratio
  • Reduce cooking time by 10-15% when increasing portions significantly
  • For baking, keep leavening agents (baking powder, yeast) at original ratio
  • Always round measurements to practical values (e.g., 2.9 cups → 3 cups)
  • Consider pan size when scaling - you may need multiple pans
