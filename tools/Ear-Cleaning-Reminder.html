Ear Cleaning Information

Regular ear cleaning is essential for your pet's health and comfort:

  • Prevents ear infections and mites
  • Removes dirt, wax, and debris
  • Improves hearing and comfort
  • Allows early detection of problems
Important: This reminder provides general ear care recommendations. Never use cotton swabs inside the ear canal. Consult your veterinarian if you notice redness, swelling, discharge, or odor. Some breeds require more frequent cleaning.

Most pets need ear cleaning every 1-2 months unless they have floppy ears, swim frequently, or have a history of ear problems. Dogs with upright ears typically need less frequent cleaning than those with floppy ears. Always use pet-safe ear cleaning solutions.
